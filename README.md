# Swiggy-Orders-Analytics---SQL-and-Power-BI-Project
This Project mainly focused on analyzing Swiggy food delivery order data using SQL and Power BI to derive the actionable business insights.
The main goal is to evaluate revenue performance, customer retention, delivery effiency and cancelltion impact.

**Business Objectve**
1. Analyze total revenue trends
2. Identify top performing cities and restaurants
3. Measure customer retention and repeat behaviour
4. Evaluate delivery performance
5. Determine the cancellation rate

**Tools and technologies**
1. SQL
2. Power BI / Power query / DAX

**Key Analysis Performed**

1️⃣ Revenue & Order Analysis

Total Orders & Delivered Orders

Total Revenue (GMV)

Average Order Value (AOV)

Monthly & yearly revenue trends

2️⃣ City & Restaurant Performance

City-wise revenue contribution (%)

Top restaurants by revenue

Order volume distribution by city

3️⃣ Customer Analytics

New vs Repeat customer analysis

Customer retention rate

4️⃣ Delivery & Cancellation Analysis

Average delivery time

Cancellation rate by city

**Power BI Dashboard**
1. Interactive KPI cards
2. Time series revenue trends
3. city and restaurant performance visuals
4. customer retention donut chart

**Key Business Insights**
1. Repeat customers drive higher GMV than one time customers
2. Higher delivery times correlate with increased cancellation rate
